Car guidance for new drivers

What is our aim?

This website is present to assist newly passed drivers. The target group are around the age of 17 to 25 years old, who have just passed their driving test and are now in need of a reliable car.

What kind of car do I need though?

This is the kind of question you'll be asking yourself, and with the help of this website we can look at the different areas of choosing the right car for you, reviewing all the variables that determines an ideal car for a newly passed driver.
